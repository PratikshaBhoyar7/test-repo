Specialized Nutritious Foods

Fortified Blended Foods (FBFs)

  • Blends of precooked cereals, soya, beans fortified with micronutrients
  • Provides 380Kcal per 100g with 18% protein content
  • Used in supplementary feeding and mother/child health programs :cite[4]

Ready-to-Use Foods (RUFs)

  • Contains vegetable fat, milk powder, malt dextrin (534Kcal/100g)
  • Plumpy'Doz costs $0.20 per ration
  • For prevention/treatment of moderate malnutrition :cite[4]

High Energy Biscuits

  • 450Kcal with 10-15g protein per 100g
  • Used when cooking facilities are scarce
  • Costs $0.12 per 100g packet :cite[4]
